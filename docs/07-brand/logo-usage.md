# ALITAPTAP Logo Usage Guide

## Core Mark
- Primary mark: owl symbol on yellow rounded-square field.
- Keep logo shape unskewed and unrotated.

## Variants
- Primary: `logo_primary.png`
- Light background safe: `logo_bg_light.png`
- Dark background safe: `logo_bg_dark.png`
- Navy background safe: `logo_bg_navy.png`

## Clear Space and Sizing
- Minimum clear space: at least 12% of logo width on all sides.
- Minimum digital size: 24px height for UI usage.
- Preferred app icon source size: 1024x1024.

## Brand Colors
- Sun Yellow: `#FFC700`
- Glow Yellow: `#FFE15A`
- Warm Ivory: `#F7F4DE`
- Deep Navy (support): `#0B1B3A`
- Near Black (support): `#101418`

## Do / Do Not
- Do keep strong contrast behind yellow tones.
- Do use generated background-safe variants when uncertain.
- Do not recolor the owl illustration.
- Do not add strokes, shadows, or filters beyond approved assets.
- Do not crop the rounded-square mark for icons.
